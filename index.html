THE STRUCTURED COMPETITIVE LOOP

A framework that connects every part of your season.

Like the CAGED system for guitar — FoilIQ doesn't give you more drills to practice in isolation. It gives you the connective framework that makes everything you already know work together.

01

Walk in with a game plan

FoilIQ analyzes your opponent's SWOT profile to generate a 3-part game plan: your opening tactic, the trigger that tells you when to switch, and a single mental cue to repeat under pressure. No more going in blind.

02

Bridge the coach-fencer gap

The picture the coach is painting and what the fencer perceives are often completely different. The fencer thinks they understand the adjustment. They don't. FoilIQ builds the precise internal vocabulary so when the coach says "remove the blade" — the fencer knows exactly what that feels like and when to trigger it.

03

Turn every bout into data

Log the debrief in 60 seconds while memory is fresh. FoilIQ cross-references your last 5 bouts to surface recurring patterns you can't see yourself. This is the reflection habit that separates fencers who plateau from fencers who compound.

04

Show up with a mission

Never walk into practice with an empty mind again. FoilIQ generates a weekly training focus and a single daily mission — built from your real debrief data, not generic advice. Concept → Drills from multiple angles → Performance application. The Pickup Music method, applied to competitive fencing.

DECISION TRAINING

Build competitive reflexes before the tournament.

Most fencers know the drills. The gap is applying the right tactic at the right moment — under pressure, when the score is close, against someone you've never fenced before.

This is the top 32 problem. And no amount of technical drilling fixes it.

Decision Training presents real bout scenarios and trains your response before you need it for real. The fencer who has processed 500 scenarios recognizes the situation instantly. The one who hasn't is thinking when they should be moving.
